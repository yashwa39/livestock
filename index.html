System overview

From each animal to your dashboard in seconds.

Sensors attached to each animal and within the shed report live readings to a microcontroller hub. Data is pushed to the ThingSpeak cloud, where the Smart Livestock Hub turns it into simple, colour-coded insights.

🌱 Farmer friendly · 🔗 Powered by IoT & AI

The central shed hub continuously listens to wireless sensors on your cattle and in the environment. Temperature, humidity, gas levels, and movement data are streamed to the cloud, where our dashboard and AI assistant keep watch for any early signs of illness or stress.

Benefits for your farm

Healthier herds, fewer surprises.

Smart Livestock Health Monitoring Hub combines early alerts, simple visuals, and AI guidance so you can focus on taking care of your animals instead of fighting with technology.

⏰
Early disease detection
Spot fevers, stress, and abnormal behaviour before they turn into emergencies, giving you time to isolate and treat affected animals.
🛡
Reduced livestock mortality
Proactive alerts and AI explanations support vets and farmers in making faster, more confident decisions about treatment.
📈
Improved farm productivity
Comfortable sheds and healthy herds mean better milk yield, weight gain, and long‑term productivity per animal.
💰
Cost‑effective monitoring
Uses affordable IoT hardware and the ThingSpeak cloud, so even small farms can benefit from smart monitoring.
